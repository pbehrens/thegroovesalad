Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Thank you for using BeaglePrep ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. We take your privacy seriously — especially because BeaglePrep is designed to keep your preparedness data confidential.

1. Information We Collect

BeaglePrep is designed with privacy at its core. By default, all data you enter — including inventory items, locations, quantities, and notes — is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

We may collect the following limited data:

  • Crash reports and diagnostics (anonymous): If the App crashes, we may receive an anonymous crash report through your device's operating system to help us fix bugs. This report does not include any inventory data.
  • App usage analytics (anonymous): We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most often) to improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify you.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Your inventory items, quantities, locations, or any other content you enter into the App.
  • Your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Your device's precise GPS location.
  • Payment information (the App is free and does not process payments).

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited anonymous data we receive is used solely to:

  • Identify and fix bugs and crashes in the App.
  • Understand general usage patterns to prioritize new features.

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

BeaglePrep may use third-party services for crash reporting and analytics (e.g., Apple's built-in crash reporting on iOS, or Google Play's Android Vitals). These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

BeaglePrep is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Security

Because your inventory data never leaves your device, you are in full control of its security. We recommend using your device's built-in encryption and passcode/biometric lock features to protect your data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
